Parts of Nation (PAN)

Justice: Group, or groups, responsible for upholding the nation's justice system. May also include military.

Crafts: Group, or groups, responsible for crafting necessary material goods for the nation's growth.

Laborers: Field workers, miners, haulers, etc.

Politics: responsible for upholding nation's political system.

Scholars: intellectual elite of the nation.

Religion: upholders of the religious traditions of the nation

Merchants: buys and sells goods and establishes trade lines with other settlements.

Underworld: the criminal network of the nation.

(Justice, Crafts, Laborers, Politics, Scholars, Religion, Merchants, Underworld)
